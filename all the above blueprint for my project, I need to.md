Yes, you **must open and connect accounts in advance** for all platforms and tools before your no-code automations and APIs can interact with them. This is critical for workflow operations—including social media posting, API data scraping, AI avatar generation, and orchestration tools. Here’s an SOP and technical checklist tailored for your full avatar-driven, multi-language automation project.

***

## SOP: Account Opening \& API Preparation

### **Step 1: Social Media \& Platform Accounts**

- **Decide channels** (TikTok Thai, Facebook Thai, Instagram Thai, 小紅書 Chinese, YouTube Thai, etc.).
- **Open accounts** with real verified info (phone/email, name, official logo).
- **Complete profiles** with branding, avatar/profile image, local contact, “influencer” bio, and business settings for analytics/API.[^1][^2]
- **Upgrade to business/creator accounts** where possible for API access/features (especially Meta, 小紅書, YouTube).


### **Step 2: AI \& Automation Tools**

- **Register for orchestration platforms**: n8n (self-hosted or cloud), Zapier, Make.com, IFTTT, etc.[^3][^4][^5][^6]
- **Create accounts on avatar/video generators**: D-ID, HeyGen, Colossyan, KreadoAI, Synthesia, Topview.ai, etc.[^7][^8][^9][^10]
- **Sign up for API access for LLMs \& Automation**: DeepSeek, Qwen, Baidu ERNIE (Chinese AI), Google Sheets/Airtable for databases[^6][^11][^3]


### **Step 3: Authentication and API Connections**

- For each platform, **enable developer mode/APIs:**
    - TikTok, Google, Meta, 小紅書 (requires business verification)
    - Generate API keys or OAuth tokens—these will be needed in n8n, Make, etc.[^1][^3]
- **Connect accounts to orchestration platform:**
    - Log in.
    - Authorize via OAuth or API key (store securely).
    - Test connection—make a sample workflow for each.

***

## SOP: Creating Your AI Avatar \& Voice in Multiple Languages

### **Step 1: Prepare Source Files**

- **Take high-quality face photo** (neutral background, front view).[^9][^7]
- **Record 1-3 minutes of your real voice** (quiet room, use standard greeting and tech sentences for variety).[^8]
- **Optional:** Wear holiday-specific dress or use green background for easy editing.


### **Step 2: Clone Your Face and Voice**

- **Upload photo/video** to chosen generator (D-ID, HeyGen, Colossyan, etc.).
- **Upload voice recording**—most tools let you clone and synthesize your voice in 30+ languages.[^7][^8]
- **Customize avatar settings:** Dress, gestures, holiday theme, background.
- **Preview and confirm:** Generate short test videos in each target language.


### **Step 3: Integrate With Content Automation**

- **Save animated avatar templates** for recurring use.
- Delegate scripting to AI (DeepSeek, Qwen, GPT) per channel/language.
- Each automation workflow pulls avatar video as primary visual, swaps background, and updates content/theme.

***

## SOP: Workflow Deployment With No-Code Platforms

### **Step 1: Content Database**

- Prepare a Google Sheet or Airtable with columns:
    - Content topic, script, target language, avatar dress/theme, background, video link, scheduling info.[^3][^1]


### **Step 2: Automation Build**

- Set up n8n or Make workflow:
    - Connect social media accounts.
    - Pull daily scripts from news/AI.
    - Generate video content using avatar/voice AI.
    - Schedule posts and respond to engagement.
    - Save results, analytics, lead data to database.


### **Step 3: Lead Handling, CRM, and Reporting**

- Connect automation to CRM (Zoho, Hubspot, Airtable).
- Monitor responses, engagement, auto-score leads.
- Trigger meeting invitations and send reports when qualification threshold met.[^6][^1]

***

## Extra Tips \& Ideas

- **For authenticity and trust:** Use your face/avatar consistently, sometimes swapping background or outfits per holiday for relevance.
- **For API automation:** Some platforms require ongoing account validity—check periodically for expiration or re-authentication needs.[^1][^3]
- **For creative content:** Prepare multiple avatars (different facial expressions/dresses) to match topic, mood, or season.
- **For legal compliance:** Confirm copyright, privacy policies of each image/video, especially if using your personal likeness across AI platforms.[^7]
- **For efficiency:** Batch create scripts and videos weekly, then automate daily releases using scheduling features.
